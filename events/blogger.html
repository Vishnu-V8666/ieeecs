Blogger - Blog writing competition

Last Date : January 9 2022

Open to All

Prizez worth 1k

IEEE CS SBC Government Engineering college Idukki is conducting ~ BLOGGER Blog writing competition on the topic "Challenges in open security tools".

Guidelines

  • The blog should be written in about 1000-1200 words.
  • Submissions must be original and unique; copied or previously published write-ups will be disqualified..
  • Submissions must be made in PDFformat with the name as your File Name. The file size must be less than 10 MB..
  • Only typed documents will be evaluated. Handwritten documents shall be disqualified.
  • Each applicant may submit only one blog write-up.
  • The Winner is selected based on the quality of work and quantity of information
  • Submit your entries here .
